BAJA SAEINDIA is a prestigious Collegiate Design Series competition organized by SAEINDIA, with support from industry leaders like Mahindra & Mahindra and ARAI. The event challenges engineering students across India to design, build, and race durable All-Terrain Vehicles (ATVs) capable of performing in extreme off-road conditions. Held over five days, the competition includes a series of Static and Dynamic events, culminating in a demanding Endurance race. BAJA SAEINDIA offers students a hands-on platform to apply engineering knowledge, innovate under pressure, and develop vital skills in design, manufacturing, teamwork, and project management.
