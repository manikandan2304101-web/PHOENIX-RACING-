The ATV Build

Our Baja SAE ATV is designed and built from the ground up with performance, durability, and innovation in mind.

  • Electric Powertrain – Runs on a lithium-ion battery pack instead of a gasoline engine.
  • Motor – Typically a 48V/72V BLDC (Brushless DC) motor providing high torque at low speeds.
  • Eco-Friendly – Zero tailpipe emissions, making it sustainable and environmentally friendly.
  • Brakes: Hydraulic disc brakes
  • Endurance & Performance – Designed to complete long-distance endurance runs while maintaining efficiency.
  • Innovation Focus – Encourages advancements in battery technology, motor control systems, and lightweight design.
